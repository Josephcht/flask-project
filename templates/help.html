Frequently Asked Questions


Questions & Answers :)
Can i delete account whenever i want?
Yes you can! You can proceed to the navigation bar and locate the delete user page. All you have to do is enter your username and pin.
Can I change my login pin number?
Yes, you can change your login pin number. Proceed to the change pin at the manage user page.
Can I freeze my account?
In a case where you card has been misplaced or stolen, you can freeze your account by going to the lock card page.
Can I report an issue or ask questions not appeared in the FAQs?
Yes, you can do that by going to the help dropdown and click Report % Feedback. In the forms, you can report the issue or ask questions in the report text area, once you have done that, we will get back to you via email. If you have any feedback, there is a feedback textarea for you to give a feedback.
Can I give feedback?
Yes you can by proceeding to the Report & Feedback forms. You can leave the report fill blank if you have nothing to report and just give feedback which is at the bottom of the form.

Navigation help


Tips on navigating the site
Lock Card.
Step 1: Go to the Navigation bar and click Lock Card.
Step 2: Key in your username and Pin number.
Step 3: Click the lock button and your account will be locked.
Change Pin.
Step 1: Go to the Navigation bar and click on manage users.<
Step 2: Click Change Pin.
Step 3: Key in your username and your initial pin.
Step 4: Key in your new pin.
Step 5: Press the submit button and your pin will be changed.
Delete Account.
Step 1: Go to the Navigation bar and click on manage users.
Step 2: Click Delete Account.
Step 3: Key in your username and pin.
Step 4: Click the delete button and your account will be deleted.
Report issue & Provide Feedback.
Step 1: Go to the navigation bar and click help.
Step 2: Click on Report and feedback.
Step 3: Key in your first name, last name, email and provide your issue or feedback.
Step 4: Submit your form and we will get back to you!
Transfer amount to other.
Step 1: Go to the navigation bar and click transfer.
Step 2: Validate your account to gain access
Step 3: Choose your recipient and the amount to transfer
Step 4: Confirm your transaction
